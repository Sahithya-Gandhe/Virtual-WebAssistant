Wednesday Voice Assistant - Help

Here are some voice commands you can use:

  • Back to home
    • "Time" - Tells you the current time.
    • "Date" - Tells today's date.
    • "Open YouTube" - Opens YouTube.
    • "Play [song/video]" - Searches YouTube for a song or video.
    • "Open Spotify" - Opens Spotify.
    • "Open Google" - Opens Google.
    • "Search for [query]" - Searches Google for your query.
    • "Pomodoro" - Opens a Pomodoro timer.
    • "Games" - Opens a fun game.
    • "Hello" or "Hi" - Greets you.
    • "Thank you" - Wednesday will respond politely.
